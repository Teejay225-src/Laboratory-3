Roblox

5 reasons why this game is my favourite

Roblox is an online game platform and game creation system developed by Roblox Corporation that allows users to program and play games created by themselves or other users. It offers game developers to make their own desired game inside the platform where players can enjoy. You can socialize, create, interact and play.

  • it has alot of games that vary from simulators, rpgs and more.
  • You can make your own avatar depending on your style.
  • Some games have high quality and high graphics.
  • Easy to play on laptop or computer.
  • Roblox is a multiplayer game that you can play with players from around the world.
